1. Introduction

2. Information We Collect

Information you enter directly:

  • Income and expense transactions (amounts, dates, categories, notes)
  • Budget goals and category limits
  • Account names and opening balances
  • Recurring transaction settings
  • App preferences (currency, language, theme)
Important: All of the above is stored only on your device using Apple's local storage. We have no access to this data, cannot see it, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

Information collected automatically:

Data TypePurposeStored Where
App preferences (language, currency, theme) Remember your settings between sessions On your device only
Subscription status Determine which features you can access On your device + Apple's servers
Crash reports (if enabled in iOS settings) Help us fix bugs and improve stability Apple's servers (anonymised)

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location or device location data
  • Contact list, photos, or other device data
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Behavioural or analytics data beyond what Apple provides by default
✦
3. AI Financial Assistant

Finance App includes an optional AI-powered assistant (available on paid plans) powered by Anthropic's Claude API.

When you use the AI assistant: a summary of your financial data (transaction history, budget limits, spending by category) is sent to Anthropic's servers to generate a response. This data is sent in the same message as your question and is not stored by us.

What is sent to Anthropic:

  • Your question or message
  • A summary of your current month's transactions and budgets
  • Category names and spending totals (no personally identifiable information)

What is not sent:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Your exact account names or bank details
  • Any data outside the current month's summary

Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy. If you do not wish to use the AI assistant, simply do not use the AI tab — no data is sent unless you actively send a message.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described in this policy solely to:

  • Provide the core features of the app (transaction tracking, budgets, statistics)
  • Power the AI assistant when you actively use it
  • Process subscription purchases through Apple's payment system
  • Improve the app based on anonymised crash and performance data from Apple

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the Finance App service.

🔐
5. Data Storage & Security

All financial data you enter is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure local storage (AsyncStorage). This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or use the AI feature
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security (PIN, Face ID, etc.)
  • We cannot remotely access, view, or recover your data
Important: Because data is stored locally, we cannot restore your data if you lose your device or reinstall the app without first creating a backup. We strongly recommend using the Export function regularly to keep a backup copy.
💳
6. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Finance App offers optional paid subscriptions (Pro Monthly, Pro Yearly, and Lifetime). All payments are processed exclusively by Apple's App Store — we never handle or store your payment information directly.

Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

To manage, modify, or cancel your subscription, go to: iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Finance App.

8. Your Rights

You have full control over your data at all times:

  • Access: All your data is visible directly within the app at all times.
  • Export: Use Settings → Export to download a full copy of your data as JSON, CSV, or Excel.
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.
  • Portability: Exported files can be imported back at any time using Settings → Import.

If you are located in the European Union, you may have additional rights under GDPR including the right to erasure, restriction of processing, and data portability. As all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise these rights independently by deleting the app or exporting your data.
